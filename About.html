ABOUT

As my name suggests, I am Mohsin Ali, a passionate web developer hailing from Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. My journey starts when i was in 5th standard in school my teacher asked me whats your aim in your life and i said i want to become a 'software engineer', even though I had no idea what that meant! But over the next few years, I developed a real passion for coding and technology.

My childhood was already fascinated by technology, robotics, and the internet. This curiosity about the digital world grew into a strong desire to understand how software worked. Fueled by this passion, I started learning code through YouTube tutorials, unlocking the secrets of coding and its power to build websites and servers and also about understanding the coding enviroment .

Becoming a Software Engineer is not a big thing for me because there is not only one Software Engineer in the world there are so many but i don't want to become a software engineer like those peoples who just do code . My dream isn't just coding - it's about sparking an IT revolution! I want to explore new things, try to develop new things, try to give a change in the IT industry, explore unknown tech & shake up the IT industry . It's my inner desire that one day i will do, i know it's too hard & challenging . In my case there are two wishes in my life one is Money and other one is my inner desires . The harder the hurdle, the more determined i become .

After completing my matriculation, I formally pursued my interest by joining a coding institute. This structured learning environment allowed me to refine my skills and delve deeper into the world of web development. Today, I stand proudly as a front-end developer, diligently crafting the user interface and user experience of websites. Every pixel, every interaction, is a testament to the dedication and passion ignited by that crucial step taken after matriculation.

While I enjoy being a front-end developer, my dreams stretch far beyond. I see myself as a full-stack developer, mastering both the front-end and back-end of websites. This path means continuous learning and diving deeper into the world of software development, exploring backend programming, testing, debugging, hosting, and managing domains. It's a challenging journey, but one I'm excited to embark on to become a truly well-rounded software engineer.

Once I've gained more experience in full-stack web development, I'll start learning about Android apps development. Then, I'll explore software development, followed by learning about testing, debugging, hosting, domains, with other programmimg languages and more. This is my roadmap to becoming a successful software engineer.

I am confident in my ability to master programming languages and embrace new technologies within the dynamic IT landscape. This is just the beginning of my journey. With each milestone, I grow closer to my ultimate goal of becoming a full-stack developer and contributing meaningfully to the ever-evolving world of software engineering.
